# AUDIT.md

## How to run a global audit of the repo

- Be completely honest feedback about this project, do not sugarcoat your answer
- Compare all the components to industry standard for High-Mid freq trading
- Identify the weaknesses and the strength and list the TODOs with a description of the bug/gap/task
- Put all your findings in the AUDIT_REVIEW.md file of the repository, sort by priority and impact
